About

Our company produces a wide range of Hook Lift containers and platforms, lightweight and heavy-duty type, with a volume from 5 to 40 m3 according to DIN 30722 (hook height - 1570mm), SS 3021 (hook height - 1450mm) and Skips according to DIN 30720-1, DIN 30735, SS 3020, various types - symmetric and asymmetric from 3 to 15 m3.

The body, joints of load-bearing elements and captures are welded by a semiautomatic welding with a continuous seam. All components of products are technologically enhanced.

The bottom and walls of the container are made from solid steel sheets (S235JR, Hardox) with a thickness from 3 to 5 mm, which provides a minimum of welded seams.

We manufacture hook lift containers, platforms and skips according to the customer's drawings within the shortest time period.

Products of our company meet the highest demands of our customers.
